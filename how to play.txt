1. The game is played on a 3x3 grid, with each cell representing a spot on the grid.
2. Players take turns placing their symbol (X or O) on the grid.
3. Players can choose to play with each other or one player can play with computer.
4. The goal of the game is to get three of your symbols in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
5. Players can click on the grid to place their symbol inthe corresponding cell.
6. If a player gets three of their symbols in a row, they win the game.
7. If the grid is filled and no one has won, the game is a tie.
8. Players can start a new game by clicking on the "New Game" button.
9. The game will automatically switch turns between players.
10. Click on the position where you want to place your symbol.
11. Your symbol will be placed on the selected position.

Have fun playing Tic Tac Toe.
